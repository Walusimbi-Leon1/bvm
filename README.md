# BVM — Bible Verse Memorization

A beautifully designed website for memorizing Bible verses from the **SGSS Bible**. Auto-rotates through **330 carefully chosen verses** — 5 from every book of the Bible — changing every **5 minutes** in canonical order.

## Features

- **Auto-rotation** — verses change every 5 minutes, cycling through all 66 books in order
- **Manual navigation** — Previous/Next buttons, keyboard arrows (← →), or spacebar
- **Browse & Search** — Grid view of all verses with live search
- **Random verse** — jump to a random verse anytime
- **Progress tracking** — see your position through the entire Bible
- **Persistent position** — saves your place in localStorage
- **Keyboard shortcuts** — `←`/`a` previous, `→`/`d` next, `r` random, `b` browse, `Space` next
- **Dark theme** — easy on the eyes for extended memorization sessions

## Verse Selection

Each book contributes 5 of its most well-known or doctrinally significant verses, for a total of **330 verses** covering all **66 books** of the Protestant canon.

## Data Source

All verse text is from the **SGSS Bible** — a simplified, easy-English adaptation of the King James Version (KJV).

- [SGSS Bible Repository](https://github.com/Walusimbi-Leon1/sgss-bible)
- [SGSS Organization](https://github.com/Walusimbi-Leon1/sgss)

## Build

The `verses.json` data file was generated by `build-verses.py`, which downloads and parses the SGSS Bible text files from the [sgss-bible](https://github.com/Walusimbi-Leon1/sgss-bible) repository.
